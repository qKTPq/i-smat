Welcome to i-SMaT

This program aims to develop students with a rich sense of humanity, a refined sense of morality, logical thinking, a wide perspective,and social responsibility as a pioneer, a researcher or a specialist in sustainable materials and technology after completing our “International Ph.D. Collaborative Program in Sustainable Materials and Technology for Industries between Nagoya University and Chulalongkorn University”.

Goal of the Program

The i-SMaT program is developed based on the principle of sustainable development goals (SDGs)



Admission Requirements

Admission Degree's Requires

  • Master of Science degree (M.Sc.) in Materials Science, Chemical Technology and Engineering, Geology, Physics, Chemistry
  • Master of Engineering (M.Eng.) in Materials Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Mining, and Petroleum Engineering, Metallurgy, Industrial Engineering, Electrical Engineering
  • or related fields

English Proficiency Score

TOEFL (450), IELTS (4.0), CU-TEP (45), For Students with NU as a lead-university: TOEIC (600) is required

Eligibility

The program administrative committee considered to be eligible for admission

Letters

  • Two letters of Recommendation Letters from instructor and/or superordinate are required
  • Applicants must submit Statement of Purpose and all required documents

Application

  • Apply through the online application system at www.grad.chula.ac.th
  • Program ID: 3992
